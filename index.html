What is CodeBridge Academy?

CodeBridge Academy is a practical software training academy that prepares learners for internships, jobs, and real-world software careers through hands-on projects, mentorship, and career-readiness support.

Company Profile

CodeBridge Academy is a practical software development training academy focused on preparing learners for internships, employment, freelance work, and continuous professional growth in the technology sector.

The academy bridges the gap between classroom learning and workplace expectations through hands-on projects, mentorship, portfolio building, and career-readiness training.

It serves secondary school learners, university students, fresh graduates, internship seekers, junior developers, and working professionals who want to strengthen or upgrade their software development skills.

Current Challenges in Practical Assessment

Rwanda's education and employment sectors face critical gaps that demand innovation.

Manual Marking

Assessors spend hours grading manually, leading to fatigue, errors, and inconsistent scoring across institutions.

Examination Malpractice

Without robust invigilation and anti-cheating tools, exam integrity remains a significant concern.

Delayed Results

Manual processing causes weeks or months of delay, slowing down graduation and workforce entry.

Inconsistent Scoring

Different assessors apply different standards, creating unfair outcomes and unreliable competency data.

Skills Mismatch

Employers find graduates lack practical skills, revealing a gap between education outputs and industry needs.

No Competency Verification

Employers struggle to verify actual candidate skills, relying on certificates that may not reflect true ability.

Problem vs. Solution

How Qualexas bridges the gap between assessment challenges and workforce readiness.

The Problem
  • Paper-based, slow manual assessments
  • No standardized competency framework
  • Limited workforce readiness data
  • Employers can't verify practical skills
  • High operational costs for exam administration
Qualexas Solution
  • AI-powered digital practical assessments
  • Standardized competency scoring & rubrics
  • Real-time skills analytics & workforce data
  • Verifiable digital certificates & competency reports
  • Automated grading with anti-cheating safeguards

Who Benefits from Qualexas?

TVET Schools & Universities

Automate practical exams, reduce administrative burden, and generate instant competency reports.

Employers

Access verified candidate competency profiles and make data-driven hiring decisions with confidence.

Certification Bodies

Issue tamper-proof digital certificates backed by objective assessment data and AI evaluation.

Government Institutions

Gain national-level workforce readiness insights and skills gap analytics for policy planning.

Future Vision

Becoming a Competency Intelligence Ecosystem

Qualexas aims to evolve into a comprehensive Competency Intelligence Ecosystem that connects learners, educators, employers, and policymakers through real-time skills data, AI-driven insights, and verifiable credentialing — creating a transparent, merit-based workforce marketplace for Rwanda and beyond.

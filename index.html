About Us

What is CodeBridge Academy?

CodeBridge Academy is a practical software training academy that prepares learners for internships, jobs, and real-world software careers through hands-on projects, mentorship, and career-readiness support.

Company Profile

CodeBridge Academy is a practical software development training academy focused on preparing learners for internships, employment, freelance work, and continuous professional growth in the technology sector.

The academy bridges the gap between classroom learning and workplace expectations through hands-on projects, mentorship, portfolio building, and career-readiness training.

It serves secondary school learners, university students, fresh graduates, internship seekers, junior developers, and working professionals who want to strengthen or upgrade their software development skills.

Mission

To equip learners with practical software development skills, real project experience, and career readiness needed to succeed in internships, employment, and technology innovation.

Vision

To become a leading software development academy that transforms learners into confident, industry-ready professionals who create impactful digital solutions.

Brand Promise

At CodeBridge Academy, every learner gains practical skills, builds real projects, and moves closer to internship and career success.
